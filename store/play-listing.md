## Full description (4000 chars max)

**Range-ready ballistics that work with zero signal.**

Drop Calculator is a fast, clean bullet-drop calculator built for hunters and
precision shooters. Pick your cartridge and factory load, set your zero, and get
your dial-up in MOA and MIL with turret click counts — plus a full dope card you
can read at a glance.

**100% offline.** No account. No ads. No tracking. Your data never leaves your phone.

**87 CARTRIDGES, REAL FACTORY LOADS**
Rimfire to .50 BMG — 22 LR, 17 HMR, 223/5.56, 300 Blackout, 6.5 Creedmoor, 6.5 PRC,
270 Win, 308 Win, 30-06, 7mm Rem Mag, 300 Win Mag, 338 Lapua, 45-70, 350 Legend,
450 Bushmaster, plus pistol calibers (9mm, 10mm, 44 Mag, and more). Every load uses
manufacturer-published velocity and ballistic coefficient.

**REAL PHYSICS, VALIDATED**
An embedded RK4 point-mass solver with the exact standard G1 and G7 drag tables —
validated to ~0.2 inch / 0.01 MOA against an independent reference solver out to
1000 yards. This is the same math class the serious desktop solvers use, not a
lookup table.

**BUILT FOR THE FIELD**
• Full dope card: drop, MOA, MIL, velocity, energy at every distance
• Trajectory chart with transonic warning
• Save your rifles: cartridge + load + zero + sight height, one tap to switch
• Custom loads from your chronograph data (or import a LabRadar/Garmin Xero CSV)
• Back up and restore your rifles with a file or a copy/paste code — never lose
  your dope when you change phones
• Simple mode for a fast answer; Expert mode when you need precision

**EXPERT MODE (PRO)**
• Wind drift (speed + clock direction)
• Full atmosphere: temperature, pressure, altitude, humidity — with live density
  altitude
• Configurable turret clicks (1/4, 1/8, 1/2 MOA; 0.1, 0.05 MIL)
• Truing: enter your real observed drop and the solver corrects to your rifle
• Export dope cards to PDF, CSV, or clipboard

One-time purchase. No subscription.

**Always verify your dope with live fire.** All solutions are estimates computed
from manufacturer-published data; your rifle, ammunition lot, and conditions will
differ.
