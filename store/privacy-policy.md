# Privacy Policy — Drop Calculator

**Effective date: July 7, 2026**

Drop Calculator ("the app") is a ballistics estimation tool published by Peter Davis.

## The short version

**We don't collect your data.** Drop Calculator has no accounts, no analytics, no ads,
no tracking, and no server of ours that your data is sent to. Everything you enter stays
on your device.

## What the app stores, and where

- **Rifle profiles, saved loads, settings, and preferences** are stored locally on your
  device (browser localStorage / app WebView storage). They never leave your device
  unless you explicitly export them.
- **Backups you export** (files or backup codes) are created on demand and saved where
  you choose. You control them entirely.
- **Chronograph CSV files you import** are read locally to compute averages; the file
  contents are not transmitted anywhere.

## What we do NOT do

- No personal information is collected, sold, shared, or transmitted.
- No location, contacts, camera, microphone, or device-identifier access.
- No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
- No background network activity. The app works fully offline.

## Purchases

If you purchase the optional Pro upgrade, the transaction is processed by the store
platform (Google Play) or our web payment provider. We receive only what is needed to
validate the purchase (e.g., a license status). We do not receive or store your payment
details. The platform's own privacy policy governs the transaction.

## Children

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the app's website and the
effective date above will be updated.
