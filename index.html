Specs are manufacturer-published (test-barrel velocities) — your barrel will differ, especially short barrels: a 16" AR runs ~150–200 fps under the 20" test numbers; rimfire varies barrel to barrel. Chronograph your gun and use Custom for real dope.

Clicks: MOA assumes ¼-MOA turret; MIL assumes 0.1-MIL. Sight height matters: scoped bolt gun ≈ 1.5–1.8", AR-15 ≈ 2.6". Conditions: 59°F / 29.92 inHg / sea level. Negative dial ("DN") means the bullet is still above the sight line — hold or dial down.
