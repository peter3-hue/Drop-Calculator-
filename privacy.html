PRIVACY POLICY

Drop Calculator
Effective date: July 7, 2026 · Publisher: Peter Davis

The short version

We don't collect your data. Drop Calculator has no accounts, no analytics, no ads, no tracking, and no server of ours that your data is sent to. Everything you enter stays on your device.

What the app stores, and where

  • Rifle profiles, saved loads, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device (browser localStorage / app WebView storage). They never leave your device unless you explicitly export them.
  • Backups you export (files or backup codes) are created on demand and saved where you choose. You control them entirely.
  • Chronograph CSV files you import are read locally to compute averages; the file contents are not transmitted anywhere.

What we do not do

  • No personal information is collected, sold, shared, or transmitted.
  • No location, contacts, camera, microphone, or device-identifier access.
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • No background network activity — the app works fully offline.

Purchases

If you purchase the optional Pro upgrade, the transaction is processed by the store platform (Google Play) or our web payment provider. We receive only what is needed to validate the purchase (e.g., a license status). We do not receive or store your payment details. The platform's own privacy policy governs the transaction.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page and the effective date above will be updated.
